Seungri

Lee SeungHyun, born December 12, 1990, or better known as Seungri is the main dancer and the lead vocals for Big Bang. He was born in Gwangju, South Korea and at a young age, he realised he wanted to be a singer and watched a Britney Spears and N Sync join concert on TV. He claims that if it was not for music that shapes who he is now, he would be a kindergarten teacher. As the youngest member of Big Bang, he has the least space to hang his clothes in the preparation room.

Prior to joining the group, back in Gwangju, he was the leader of the dance group "Il Hwa". He first appeared on television on the reality TV series Let's Cokeplay: Mnet Battle Shinhwa, a show in which Shinhwa attempted to create a boy band that could be a "second Shinhwa". Due to his lack of vocal skills, this led him to being eliminated from the band. If he had not been eliminated, he would have debuted in "Battle" a group of 6 boys that each Shinhwa member had selected. He was later scouted by YG Entertainment and joined the group Big Bang. Although he was initially cut from the series, he was given another chance to impress YG Entertainment CEO Yang Hyun Suk and succeeded. Back when he was part of his dance group in his hometown, he would compete against his fellow band member Taeyang in several dance competitions as well as Super Junior's Eunhyuk, TVXQ's Yunho and Shinee's Taemin. During Big Bang Documentary, for the battle between 2 teams, his team consisted of Taeyang, T.O.P. and were up against G-Dragon, Daesung and Hyunseung. Seungri was the leader of this group due to his dance skills. With an outstanding performance and clothing they had worn, they won the dance battle.

Seungri debuted in 2006 along with the other four members of Big Bang; Daesung, T.O.P., Taeyang and G-Dragon. He recorded his first solo "The Next Day" in 2007 which was included in Big Bang's first album Since 2007. Seungri became the first member of Big Bang to act in a musical with the perfornamce in Sonagi in 2008. His next musical was performed alongside bandmate Daesung in 2009 "Shouting", who was unable to perform due to a car accident. Although his first solo song "Next Day" was recorded as part of Big Bang's album, his second song "Strong Baby" was released as a promotional single for the group's full-length album Remember. In order to get rid of the "youngest member" look from Big Bang, Seungri promoted the single with a more mature look along with a stylized dance. He won his first triple crown fro "Strong Baby" on Inkigayo.

In July 2012, Seungri started his 1st Japanese Promotions by appear on a variety of shows such as Yomiuri TV's Yoshimoto Jounetsu Comedy, Run for Money etc. He was interviewed along with many popular stars such as Oguri Shun, Exile's Akira, Miori Takimoto, takei Emi, Perfume, and even senior Se7en and his own bandmates. Seungri in 2013 resided in Japan along with bandmate Daesung for Japanese Promotions. On July 28, 2013, it was annouced by YG Entertainment that Seungri has been secretly preparing a 2nd mini album that was released in August that year. The track title being "할말 있어요" (I Have Something To Say). HE released his 1st Japanese album on October 9th in that year which contains songs from Let's Talk About Love as well as songs from his first mini album, VVIP, all of which were recorded in Japanese. On February 28, 2014, YG Entertainment annouced that Seungri will star on the new SBS weekend drama 'Angel Eyes' alongside labelmate Ku Hye-sun. Seungri will play the role of Teddy who returns to Korea after living abroad with the dream of becoming a 119 rescue worker. Teddy is a cheerful, straightforward and innocent character who has a sense of duty for saving lives and falls in love for a florist at first sight.
